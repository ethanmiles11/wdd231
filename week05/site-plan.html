1. Site name:

Galaxy Guide (GalaxyGuide.net)

-This name shows that the site is "space" themed. It helps the user understand that this is a website to help educate and learn about our galaxy.

2. Site Purpose:

Galaxy Guide is an interactive Space website that lets users explore planets and our galaxy. It offers planet profiles, space facts, and a visual galaxy map, making space exploration fun and educational.

3. Scenarios:

-What makes each planet unique?

-Which planets have been explored by spacecraft?

4. Color Schema:

-Russian Violet - Will be used as the primary color for backgrounds.

-Ultra Violet - Will be used for headings, accents, buttons, etc.

-White - Used for almost all text except in specific instances.

-Night - used for background and accents to provide extra "space" feel.

5. Typography:

Roboto Regular - Roboto regular will be the text throughout most of the site for paragraphs/modals

Audiowide - May be used for the title or main heading on the page. It's a futuristic "space"-like feel.
